The estimation is based on average values. In many cases Arbitral Institutions may calculate fees differently taking into account all the parameters of a particular case.


The estimation does not cover:

‣ the arbitrators’ expenses
‣ the parties’ expenses
‣ experts' fees and expenses
‣ interpreters’ fees and expenses
‣ hearing room rental fees

The estimation is based on the following rules:

‣ AIAC — 2018 Arbitration Rules and 2018 Fast Track Arbitration Rules
‣ CIETAC — 2015 Arbitration Rules
‣ DIS — 2018 Arbitration Rules
‣ HKIAC — 2018 Arbitration Rules
‣ ICAC — 2017 International Arbitration Rules
‣ KCAB — 2016 International Arbitration Rules
‣ RAC — 2019 Arbitration Rules
‣ RSPP — 2018 Arbitration Rules
‣ SCC — 2017 Arbitration Rules and 2017 Expedited Arbitration Rules
‣ SIAC — 2016 Arbitration Rules
‣ VIAC — 2018 Arbitration Rules

All values are in US dollars. Exchange rates are updated once an hour.
